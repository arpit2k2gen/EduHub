FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Que 1: Who are we

Ans. We are one of the non profitable organizations which works for the upliftment and welfare of the under-priviledged students and children across the country, by either funding their educations or by helping the other organisations who runs the special schools for these children.


Que 2. How we help the students

Ans. We as a organisation donate and help the students directly by funding their education, or bu either donating to other organisations which run special schools for these children where they are provided with the free educations. We donate them money, accessories and other stuff.


Que 3. In which regions of India do we run our charity and donations

Ans. We run our donations and charity in every region of our country. We provide donations tos every foundation, every organisation, every special school and we hope soon to every worthy children even.
