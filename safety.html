SAFETY AND RIGHTS

Being aware about your safety and rights is very important. Here are some of the important Rights and Basic safety tips:


SAFETY TIPS
  1. You must stand against the bullying and the ragging as it is becoming very common now-a-days. If you are facing such in any institute/school, you must approach the higher dignitaries of that particular institutes/school. If it doesn't work, you must report your problem to anti-ragging cell of India,
  2. You must stand against any harassment being faced by you or any of the student in your institution. You must report this problem to the higher authorities or you must report this problem to your parents. if that doesn't help you must file a legal action against that person in your nearest police station.
  3. If any teacher or anyother authority is being biased to you or anyone else, you must report this problem to your parents and higher dignitaries of your institution.

BASIC RIGHTS
  1. Every children has right to get education in this country at any state, place or institution. No one can deny him to get education.
  2. No child could be discriminated on the basis of his caste, color and race. No one can deny him for getting admission on basis of any discrimination.
  3. Every physicaly/mentally disabled child has right to educate in normal schools of this country. No one can deny his/her admission with regards to his disabilities.
